Tareen Education Foundation

is a nonprofit organisation with a mandate to improve quality of education in Pakistan; primarily operating in the rural areas of Southern Punjab. Tareen Education Foundation (TEF) is a nonprofit organization established under The Societies Registration.

OUR VISION

Our objective is to create centres of learning, which provide students an enabling environment to develop their faculties of critical analysis and independent thinking, to polish their communication and articulation skills, to increase their understanding of social issues, and to make them active.

OUR MISSION

TEF is a non-profit organization with a mandate to provide quality education, especially to the marginalized communities, such as the rural poor and women. Act of 1860 with a mandate to improve quality of education in Pakistan
